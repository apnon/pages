FAQ

OroCommerce demo questions

Is this OroCommerce demo free?

Yes. This OroCommerce demo is completely free and ungated. Open it and log in with the buyer account, with no signup and no sales call. It is hosted by Synolia and resets every night.

Is this OroCommerce Community or Enterprise edition?

It showcases OroCommerce Enterprise, hosted and managed by Synolia. You are seeing the enterprise B2B capabilities: corporate account hierarchies, CPQ-style quoting, customer price lists and advanced approvals.

Do I need to register?

No. There is no registration to use the demo. Log in with buyer@meridian-demo.com and the shared password to explore B2B pricing, quotes and approvals. You only share your details if you want a personalized demo on your own catalog.

What can I test as a buyer?

As a buyer you can browse the catalog, see customer-specific and tier pricing, build a quick order, request a quote, and run an order through the approval workflow, all on the fictional Meridian catalog.

How do I see the back office?

The public demo is storefront-only. Full OroCommerce back-office access, on your own products and pricing, comes with a personalized demo that Synolia builds for you within 24 hours.
