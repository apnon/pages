Questions you're probably asking

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, free. The honest catch: we hope that once you see what's possible on your own business, some of you will want to talk about a real project. Most won't, and that's fine. No obligation, no pressure.

Do I have to share my real prices?

No. We never ask for actual prices — only the structure of your pricing (customer lists, volume breaks, quotes…) so the demo behaves like your business. You pick from common models in the form.

What about my data and my catalog?

Whatever you share is used only to build your demo, stored securely, never shared, and deleted within 30 days of your walkthrough. Ask us to delete it sooner at any time.

What do I actually keep afterward?

The recording of the live walkthrough, a screenshot pack of the build, and documentation of what we built and how. Concrete material you can take into internal meetings and iterate on.

What if my catalog data is messy?

Totally normal. Send whatever you have — a PDF catalog, an Excel export, even a link to your current site. We extract what we can. Messy data is the reality we handle every day.
