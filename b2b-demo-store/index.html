Questions you're probably asking

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, free. The "catch" is honest: we hope that once you see what's possible on your own business, some of you will want to talk about a real project. Most won't, and that's fine. You're under no obligation, and we never pressure you.

Do I have to share my real prices?

No. We never ask for your actual prices. We only need to understand the structure of your pricing (for example: customer-specific lists, volume breaks, quotes) so the demo behaves like your business. You pick from common models in the form.

What about my data and my catalog?

Whatever you share is used only to build your demo, stored securely, never shared with anyone, and deleted within 30 days of your walkthrough. You can ask us to delete it sooner at any time.

Why does it take a week?

Because it's real. A team has to ingest your catalog, set up a live B2B platform, model your pricing and prepare your scenarios. We need your details at least 3 business days before the date so we can do it properly.

What if I don't have clean catalog data?

Totally normal. Send whatever you have — a PDF catalog, an Excel export, even a link to your current site. We extract what we can. Messy data is part of the reality we're used to handling.
