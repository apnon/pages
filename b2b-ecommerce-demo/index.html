FAQ

Questions about the demos

Are these B2B ecommerce demos free?

Yes. Every demo is free to open and use, with no signup and no sales call. They are always-on storefronts hosted by Synolia, and each one resets every night.

Do I need to sign up or talk to sales?

No. There is no gate. Open any platform demo and log in with the shared buyer account to see B2B pricing, quotes and approvals. You only share your details if you want a personalized demo on your own catalog.

Why is the same catalog on every platform?

Every demo runs the same fictional catalog, Meridian Industrial Supply. Holding the data constant means the only thing that changes between demos is the platform itself, so you compare platforms rather than data sets.

Can I see the back office?

The public demos are storefront-only, with a buyer login. Full back-office access, on your own products and pricing, comes with a personalized demo that Synolia builds for you within 24 hours.

Which platform is right for me?

OroCommerce Enterprise suits companies that need deep, built-in enterprise B2B: corporate account hierarchies, CPQ-style quoting and advanced approvals. Medusa suits teams that want a modern headless stack, composable and API-first, with no per-seat license cost. A personalized demo and a one-hour expert walkthrough are the fastest way to decide.

How do I get a demo with my own catalog?

Fill in the short personalized-demo form and pick your platform. Synolia builds a live demo with your brand, catalog and pricing within 24 hours, including back-office access, and proposes a one-hour walkthrough. No strings attached.
